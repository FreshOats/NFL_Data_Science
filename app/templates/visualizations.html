How to Use This Visualization

  • Click on the arrows to switch tabs for different analyses
  • Reset the Play Time and press the forward animation button below to see the plays over time
  • Select a different Injury Key to see different plays
  • At the bottom of this page, there is a link to Tableau Public for a more user-friendly Viz



What You Are Observing

  • Each Play Key is a different play that resulted in a concussion.
  • The first panel examines the linear impulse and torque of the injured player, and the opposing team's player who was involved in the collision leading to the concussion. The path is from the start of play to the time of injury.
  • The second panel examines linear impulse and body torque or internal torque. Body torque is the twisting of the player as they turn on the field. Internal torque looks at the differece in the player's orientation and direction of movement as the player twists their body.
  • The third panel examines the torques with linear impulse, but includes directionality, allowing you to see as they turn from left to right or vice versa.
